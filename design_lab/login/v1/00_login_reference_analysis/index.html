Reference Analysis

Login Screen Breakdown

This study captures the structure, typography, spacing, and interaction language of the supplied login reference before we adapt it into Stitchly’s own login system.

Overall Composition

High-level read
  • Near-black full-screen environment with a luxury, editorial feel.
  • Strict split-screen composition with a brand stage on the left.
  • Rounded login card inset on the right rather than full-bleed form layout.
  • Very large title, very small helper text, and generous empty space.
Primary layers
  • Outer app frame with minimal padding.
  • Left brand panel with wordmark, symbol, and guide lines.
  • Right login card with internal form composition.
  • Detached circular CTA anchored to the bottom-right of the card.

Typography

Observed type hierarchy
  • Oversized page title with light or regular weight.
  • Small field labels that stay visually quiet.
  • Larger entered values that become the main scan target.
  • Tiny secondary actions like account creation and forgot password.
Practical implications
  • The title carries most of the emotional weight.
  • Labels should never compete with input values.
  • Support text should be restrained and low-emphasis.
  • We should pick a clean sans with premium editorial presence.

Spacing And Rhythm

Spacing traits
  • Large title-to-form gap.
  • Tight label-to-underline spacing.
  • Small field-row to helper-row spacing.
  • Large empty lower-middle card area.
  • Detached CTA with intentional breathing room.
Why it feels premium
  • Nothing is crowded.
  • Empty space is treated as a design tool.
  • Alignment feels strict and confident.
  • There are very few competing primitives on screen.

Element Inventory

Left-side stage
  • Top-left wordmark.
  • Central abstract monument/symbol.
  • Faint guide/grid lines.
  • Tiny footer/legal copy.
Right-side form
  • Top-right secondary action.
  • Large page title.
  • Email field and password field in one row.
  • Remember-me control and forgot-password link.
  • Bottom-right circular sign-in CTA.

Stitchly Adaptation

Preserve
  • Split-screen shell.
  • Right-side rounded login card.
  • Oversized title hierarchy.
  • Quiet support actions.
  • Spacing discipline and asymmetrical CTA placement.
Replace
  • Golden Suisse branding with Stitchly brand assets.
  • Finance-style left monument with workflow/canvas symbolism.
  • Pure monochrome emphasis with selective lava-core accents.
  • Banking tone with workflow/editor/product tone.
