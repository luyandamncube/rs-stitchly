Why separate

Reading checkpoint state should stay explicit. It gives operators a clear place to understand whether a recurring sync is truly incremental or effectively bootstrapping again.

Design direction

This node should feel like control-plane state, not data-plane movement. Users are asking “where are we resuming from?” here.
