Why separate

A merge finishing does not automatically mean the data is fit for downstream use. Keeping quality explicit makes the “written” versus “trusted” boundary visible.

Design direction

This node should feel operator-facing and reviewable. It is where users decide whether the newly ingested slice is safe enough to advance pipeline state.
